WHO IS PRODUCEMART?

Producemart.com is a brand new online B2B marketplace for wholesale agricultural produce and ingredients. It aims to make it simpler than ever to list and source produce at a local, regional or international level.
Learn More

HOW IT WORKS

Find out how you can start supplying or buying on Producemart in 9 easy steps.

1

Supplier undergoes Audit process to become a verified supplier

2

Supplier uploads product to ProduceMart

3

Buyer finds product and request a quote

4

Supplier & Shipper confirm product availability and respond with a quote

5

If a Buyer is happy to proceed, they pay into escrow to show supplier they are commited to the transaction

6

All orders are inspected by our 3rd party inpector partners - SGS and if the quantity and quality of the product are verified, the order is released to the shipper for forwarding

7

Shipper confirms receipt of order and releases airway bill or Bill of laden

8

ProduceMart releases payment to supplier

9

Order received by buyer
